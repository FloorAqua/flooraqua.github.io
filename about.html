The FloorAqua Philosophy

We believe that a premium, hospital-grade clean shouldn't be a luxury reserved for the elite. Our vision is to bridge the gap between high-end quality and affordability, ensuring every home can enjoy professional-standard hygiene without the premium price tag.

🌱

A Homegrown Promise

"Great things start from humble beginnings."

FloorAqua didn't start in a massive corporate factory; it started right here at home in Moradabad. Founded by Anurag Masih, an alumnus of Green Meadows School and a student at MJPRU University, our goal is to bring personal care, scientific excellence, and safe cleaning products directly from our family to yours.

🤝

Unmatched Support

Because we are a local business, we provide a commitment you won't get from big brands. From instant order processing to personalized customer care, we treat every customer like a premium partner.
